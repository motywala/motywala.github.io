Project Proposal

Web Based School Management System

Description

  • A Web-based School Management System, that serves the purpose for providing the students and teachers, a full-fledge and responsive management platform that caters major management and record-handling issues for teachers and academic and course-related issues for the students; both in a well-organized and functional manner.

    • Teacher Attendance Management

    • Teacher Transport Management
